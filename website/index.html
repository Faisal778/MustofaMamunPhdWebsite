I am originally from Bangladesh. I completed my bachelor's degree in Urban Planning. While studying Urban Planning, I became interested in economics.

My research primarily focuses on fiscal policy and macro issues. Currently, I am working on estimating the size of fiscal multipliers using alternative statistical methods and teaching Basic Microeconomics at Fordham University.
